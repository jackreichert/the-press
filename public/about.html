Privacy

The Press does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no cookies, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Your game progress (found words, score) is saved only in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device. Clearing your browser data removes it.

The only network requests this game makes are to fetch the puzzle schedule and dictionary when you open it.

This site is served via Cloudflare. Cloudflare may collect standard request metadata (IP address, country, timestamp) for network security purposes. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.
